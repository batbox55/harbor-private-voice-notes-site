Availability

Harbor is built for iPhone and requires iOS 26 or later. The App Store product link will be added here when Harbor’s listing is available.

Common questions

Where are my recordings?

Harbor stores each recording in its own folder inside the Apple Files location you selected. If you chose iCloud Drive, Apple handles syncing through your Apple account. Harbor does not keep a developer-operated cloud copy.

How do I delete Harbor data?

Delete a note in Harbor or remove its recording folder in Apple Files. Separately exported files remain wherever you saved them.

Harbor cannot record or transcribe

Open iOS Settings, select Harbor, and check Microphone and Speech Recognition permissions. Location permission is optional. Some on-device features may download model files the first time they are used.

Can Harbor process a phone call?

Harbor does not record calls directly. On supported iPhones and in available regions, tap More (+), then Call Recording during a call. iPhone saves the recording in Notes; choose Save Audio Files to place a copy in Apple Files, then import and process it in Harbor. Everyone on the call hears a recording notice. See Apple’s call-recording guide.

What should I include in a bug report?

Describe what you expected, what happened, your iPhone model, and your iOS version. Do not email private recordings or transcripts unless you intentionally choose to share them.
